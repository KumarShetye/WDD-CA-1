Why Climate Action is Critical?

By Kumar Shetye





Climate Action is critical due to the vitality of the planet laying in our own hands. Through each generation who walks this soil, its our actions in the present moment that determine the kind of world we will pass onto the future generation, our kids, our grandkids. Looking after the environment isn't just an environmental responsibility, however its our moral duty, that we must look after this planet better than the way we found it and ensure the future generations will thrive in a healthy and sustainable planet.

The temperatures are rising critically melting the ice caps and destroying the habitats for the wildlife species and animals such as these poor penguins. As the ice melts, the sea levels rise and the animals who have adapted to the cold will struggle to survive in this changing world. Pushing many of them to extinction. It is essential that we protect the vulnerable ecosystems and maintain the delicate balance that of which is supporting both the wildlife and the human life.





This climate change is not a distant threat, its here and time is ticking day by day. The longer we continue to walk this path of delayed action the more costly it will become to reverse the damages that are caused by the greenhouse gas emissions and the extreme weather events. If we act now, we can hinder the effects of climate change, save the coastal communites and prevent the irreversible harm towaeds the planet that we depend on.
